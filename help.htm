Online Help:

The Site Selector application allows individuals and businesses to explore a region and discover information that can aid in business decision-making.

Finding Information

Users of the Site Selector application can identify buildings or sites that are for sale or lease that may meet specific business needs. The application can also be used to assess the existing business landscape, as well as explore information about the surrounding community.

Map Based Navigation

There are several ways to interact with the map:

1. Tap a point of interest you'd like to see
2. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
3. Hold down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
4. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out

Searching

The search box can be used to find an address, road name, or county:

1. Enter the address into the Search box
2. Enter the distance from that address within which you want to search
3. Click on any of the results in the panel or any of the items on the map to get more information
